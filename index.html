The Rubber Plant

Rubber plants are grown for their glossy, leathery, large leaves. The leaves are oblong to oval, 8 to 12 inches long and in various colors of dark green, deep maroon or marked with yellow, cream, pink or white.

Basics

Light
It requires least six to eight hours of light daily.
Cost
Average cost of rubber planting material is 75 rupees per piece.
Water
It requires least six to eight hours of light daily.
Care
Keep their soil slightly moist, make sure they get plenty of sunshine.
